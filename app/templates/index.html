Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our funding process

How fast can I get funded?

Most applications are approved within hours, and funds are typically deposited within 24 hours of approval. In urgent cases, we can expedite same-day funding.

What are the minimum requirements?

Your business should be operational for at least 6 months with minimum monthly revenue of $10,000. We consider various factors beyond credit score.

Do you check personal credit?

We review personal credit as part of our evaluation, but we focus more on your business performance and cash flow. Many clients with less-than-perfect credit have been approved.

Is there a prepayment penalty?

No, there are no prepayment penalties. You can pay off your advance early without any additional fees, potentially saving on factor costs.
